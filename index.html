Let's break it down...

An Epic Quest

It's called a challenge because, well... it's hard. It's supposed to be. This challenge requires discipline, effort, endurance, and courage. If you choose to accept this quest, you will find struggles along the way. You will also find it extremely rewarding!

Publish

Not just a draft, or an idea... actually click the publish button and send it to the internet. This can be scary, but that's why we call it a challenge.

Promote

Ok it's live... now make sure that people actually know about it!

  • Make a public post on your social networks
  • Send a private message to at least 2 people who would benefit from/enjoy your post

30 Pieces of Content

Choose written, audio, or video. I recommend written since it's easiest. Keep it simple!

30 Days

Publish & Promote one every. single. day.

On A Blog You Own

This is not submitting 30 articles to an online magazine. It's not writing 30 long posts on Facebook. This is about publishing on a website you control so that you can guarantee it gets published, build your authority, attract an audience, and own your content.

üì¢ Frequently Asked Questions

When Does It Start?

The challenge is always happening with other challengers around the world. It's an individual journey, so you can start whenever you're ready.

Choose when you want to begin, mark, your calendar, and go for it.

If this sounds like it's for you... prepare today and start tomorrow!

Do Have To?

Only if you want the benefits.

Where do I share my posts once they've been published?

Share your newly published post of the day to any social network you're active on: ie. Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, etc. Ideally all of them!

Then send your private message thru anywhere the other people are likely to get it: ie. email, FB messenger, Twitter DM, Instagram DM, etc.

Can I Do 30 Instagram Posts or 30 YouTube Videos Instead?

Ultimately it's up to you. The important part is to publish every day and share your work.

If you're really focused on building your YouTube channel, this makes sense. Just consider your goals. Most of the time building your own platform will take you further in the long run.

Can I Make My Content Ahead of Time?

You can create 10 pieces of content in a day if you really want, just publish and promote one every single day.

It's highly beneficial to spend a day or two per week where you batch your content and work on drafts of your content. Why? Staring at a blank page at 11:59pm on a day when you need to publish is not a fun place to be. Life happens. Being a day or two ahead can really relieve som stress.

However, don't plan on doing ALL of your content ahead of time. Why? Well, for starters, that's a lot of content. And if you're a perfectionist and over-achiever and you think you'd want to do that... but know that you'll never have time to create all that content ahead of time, you've now talked yourself out of doing the challenge. Don't give yourself a reason not to participate! Also, there's some magic that happens once you're partway through the process. You'll get new ideas, people will ask you for something on a particular topic. Find your own balance of how much to do ahead of time.
